# Metrics and Experiments

## North-star candidate

**Consequential actions with externally supported outcome status.**

This avoids optimizing for raw logs or signatures that do not improve assurance.

## Product metrics

- valid bundles generated;
- bundles with receiver/system-of-record evidence;
- time from action to final outcome;
- PENDING to VERIFIED conversion;
- contradiction and reversal detection rate;
- verification failure and incomplete rate;
- active projects and issuers;
- retained evidence and re-verification events;
- SDK activation and integration time.

## Commercial metrics

- qualified inbound interest;
- demo-to-integration rate;
- free-to-paid conversion;
- MRR and expansion;
- gross margin by evidence operation;
- support minutes per customer;
- churn and retention duration.

## Required early experiments

1. Non-technical refund demo comprehension.
2. Independent developer integration under ten minutes.
3. Stripe or equivalent sandbox final-outcome connector.
4. Design-partner evidence-gap interview.
5. Willingness-to-pay test for retention and re-verification.
6. AI assistant discoverability test using only public first-party pages.
