# Revenue and Pricing Plan

Status: internal hypotheses only.

## Revenue objective

Build a credible, measurable path to at least EUR 10,000 MRR without making manual consulting the default delivery model.

## Value units

Customers may pay for:

- retained evidence volume;
- verification and re-verification operations;
- managed issuers and keys;
- systems-of-record connectors;
- retention duration;
- domain rulesets and contradiction alerts;
- evidence packets and exports;
- users, projects, and environments;
- service-level and support commitments.

## Packaging hypotheses

### Community

Open protocol, schemas, local verifier, test vectors, and base SDKs.

### Developer

Managed keys, limited retention, hosted verification, and basic connector usage.

### Team or Assurance

Longer retention, multiple issuers, rulesets, alerts, exports, and team controls.

### Enterprise later

Private deployment, advanced retention, compliance exports, SSO, SLA, and custom connector governance only after operational maturity.

## Metrics before pricing

- activation: first valid externally sourced bundle;
- time to first evidence;
- verified outcomes per active project;
- percentage PENDING/INCOMPLETE/CONTRADICTED;
- retained bundles and re-verification frequency;
- connector usage;
- conversion, retention, expansion, support time, and gross margin.

## Stop conditions

Reassess the business if:

- users value only the free receipt format;
- no buyer pays for retention, connectors, or assurance;
- required integrations are predominantly custom services;
- support and liability exceed feasible pricing;
- platform-native features remove the independent value.
