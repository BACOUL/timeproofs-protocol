# Assumptions Register

An assumption is not a fact. Each assumption requires a validation method and a decision consequence.

| ID | Assumption | Status | Validation experiment | Failure consequence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A-001 | Chats and agents will execute more consequential actions directly | Strong external signal, continuing watch | Track ACP, UCP, AP2, MCP, A2A and product launches quarterly | Reduce emphasis on in-chat distribution; retain enterprise workflow evidence use case |
| A-002 | Existing traces are insufficient for business accountability | Partially validated by market entrants | Interview or observe design partners handling refunds, claims, orders, or account changes | Reposition toward narrower technical interoperability or discontinue |
| A-003 | Systems of record can provide stronger evidence than agent self-claims | Technically credible, adoption unvalidated | Build Stripe/Shopify sandbox connector and measure completeness | Use gateway-observed evidence only and narrow claim strength |
| A-004 | An open protocol improves adoption more than a closed format | Unvalidated | Compare SDK adoption and partner response to open verifier and schemas | Keep open verifier, move more product value to managed service |
| A-005 | Long-term retention and re-verification are paid needs | Unvalidated | Present packaging to design partners and measure willingness to pay | Monetize connectors, organization rules, or operational evidence workflows instead |
| A-006 | Developers will integrate evidence middleware voluntarily | Unvalidated | Five-minute SDK test with external developers | Shift distribution through platforms/connectors rather than direct SDK adoption |
| A-007 | A precise outcome vocabulary can become a cultural category moat | Aspirational | Track third-party use of TimeProofs terms and schema mappings | Treat vocabulary as open infrastructure, compete on service and integrations |
| A-008 | A lean founder can reach EUR 10,000 MRR with product-led adoption | Unvalidated | Stage conversion, activation, retention, and support-cost experiments | Seek partnerships, narrow market, or reconsider project economics |
| A-009 | Cross-protocol evidence is valuable beyond each protocol's native receipts | Unvalidated | Produce one dossier using three independent sources and test buyer value | Focus on one domain connector rather than universal bridge |
