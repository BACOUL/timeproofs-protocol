# Review Cadence

## Every work session

Read the mandatory sequence in `AGENTS.md`. Confirm the unique next action and run project validation before and after work.

## Every pull request

Review:

- decisions;
- risks and assumptions;
- compatibility impact;
- external claims;
- generated project state;
- release gate status.

## Weekly during active development

- upstream protocol changes;
- competitor product changes;
- security advisories;
- current blocker and next-action validity;
- repository and CI health.

## Monthly

- full competitor radar;
- standards and research watch;
- business and cost assumptions;
- SEO/GEO opportunity map;
- legal and regulatory watch;
- roadmap and risk review.

## Quarterly or before a major phase

- category and positioning review;
- product boundary review;
- build-versus-partner decisions;
- pricing and revenue path;
- internationalization priorities;
- architecture and threat model review;
- continue, narrow, pivot, or stop decision based on evidence.

## Event-triggered review

Immediate review is required when:

- ACP, UCP, AP2, MCP, A2A, or a major platform ships a relevant breaking feature;
- a direct competitor launches a materially overlapping capability;
- a security or privacy issue appears;
- a regulation changes product obligations;
- a design partner supplies evidence that invalidates a core assumption.
