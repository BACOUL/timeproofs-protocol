# ACP Compatibility Watch

Last reviewed: 2026-07-23

Official sources:

- https://openai.com/index/buy-it-in-chatgpt/
- https://openai.com/index/powering-product-discovery-in-chatgpt/

ACP supports AI-native product discovery and commerce interactions between ChatGPT, users, and merchant systems.

TimeProofs target:

- correlate user confirmation, checkout, merchant acceptance, order identifiers, payment references, fulfillment, return, and refund evidence;
- preserve the merchant as the source for merchant-record facts;
- avoid claiming that ChatGPT or checkout transport alone proves fulfillment or settlement.

No ACP adapter is implemented yet. Versioned mapping work begins only after the core protocol and developer gates.
