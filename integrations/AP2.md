# AP2 Compatibility Watch

Last reviewed: 2026-07-23

Primary watch surface: official Google AP2 documentation and UCP compatibility materials.

TimeProofs target:

- reference user and cart/payment mandates by digest;
- record payment receipt or authorization evidence;
- distinguish authorized, captured, settled, refunded, reversed, and disputed states;
- correlate payment evidence with the merchant action and final business outcome.

Boundary: payment authorization alone does not prove the purchased service or item was fulfilled.
