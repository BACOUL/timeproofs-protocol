# UCP Compatibility Watch

Last reviewed: 2026-07-23

Official source: https://developers.googleblog.com/under-the-hood-universal-commerce-protocol-ucp/

UCP defines open commerce primitives and integration paths across consumer surfaces, businesses, payment providers, APIs, MCP, and A2A.

TimeProofs target:

- map UCP commerce lifecycle objects into Outcome Events;
- preserve native identifiers and digests;
- connect order and fulfillment events to merchant systems of record;
- integrate AP2 mandate/payment evidence without treating authorization as settlement.

No UCP profile is implemented yet.
