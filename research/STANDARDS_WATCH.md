## Participation rule

TimeProofs should not pursue “standard” status as a branding shortcut. Standards participation begins when:

- implementation and vectors exist;
- at least one external implementation or partner has feedback;
- the proposal fills a documented interoperability gap;
- security and privacy limitations are explicit;
- participation does not displace product validation.
