# Brand and Naming System

## Company and umbrella brand

**TimeProofs**

## Product

**TimeProofs Outcome Assurance**

## Canonical objects

- Outcome Event — atomic lifecycle observation or claim;
- Evidence Bundle — portable signed machine record;
- Outcome Evidence Packet — human-readable rendering;
- Outcome Ledger — long-term multi-revision chronology;
- Structural Verifier — cryptographic and structural validation;
- Verdict Engine — versioned evidence-sufficiency evaluation.

## Badge vocabulary

Potential states, subject to demo and UX validation:

- Outcome Verified
- Verification Pending
- Evidence Incomplete
- Outcome Contradicted
- Outcome Reversed
- Outcome Unprovable

A badge MUST open or reference the underlying evidence packet and MUST NOT conceal limitations.

## Canonical slogan

> When an AI says “done”, verify what actually happened.

## Naming guardrail

Do not rename core objects casually. Public protocol names create compatibility and cultural cost. Any change requires a migration decision and source-level compatibility review.
