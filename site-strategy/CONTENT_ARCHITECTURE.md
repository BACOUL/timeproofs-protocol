## Content layers

### Layer 1 — Category education

Plain-language problem, outcome states, and comparison with traces or logs.

### Layer 2 — Working proof

Interactive demos, public verifier, human Evidence Packets, reproducible bundles.

### Layer 3 — Developer adoption

Quickstarts, SDKs, API reference, protocol mappings, schemas, error codes, test vectors.

### Layer 4 — Buyer assurance

Security, privacy, legal, retention, operations, trust center, architecture, procurement material.

### Layer 5 — Authority and evolution

Research, standards watch, competitor comparisons, changelog, roadmap, decision and source registers.

## Required cross-links

Every use-case page links to:

- the relevant demo;
- evidence semantics;
- limitations;
- integration guide;
- verifier;
- pricing only when commercially available.
