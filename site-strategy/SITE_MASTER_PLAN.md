# Global Site Master Plan

## Site mission

`timeproofs.io` must become the authoritative source for the TimeProofs product, protocol, verifier, demonstrations, trust posture, and category vocabulary.

## Migration stages

### Stage S0 — Preserve AgentReady

- Keep the current public site stable while Outcome Assurance is experimental.
- Do not replace indexed pages with unproven claims.
- Preserve AgentReady releases and documentation.

### Stage S1 — Experimental Outcome Assurance preview

Entry gate: protocol and demo foundations exist.

Possible surfaces:

- `/outcome-assurance/`;
- `/demo/refund/`;
- `/protocol/`;
- experimental qualifier on every page.

### Stage S2 — Product pivot

Entry gate: G1, G2, and G3 pass.

- Homepage becomes Outcome Assurance.
- AgentReady moves to a clearly labeled historical/community route.
- Canonical metadata, redirects, sitemap, and entity descriptions are reconciled.

### Stage S3 — Commercial global site

Entry gate: managed service and market validation.

Required pages:

- homepage;
- product and how it works;
- protocol and verifier;
- developer docs and SDKs;
- integrations;
- demonstrations and evidence packets;
- security, privacy, trust, legal, status, and responsible disclosure;
- pricing and account flows;
- use cases and comparisons;
- changelog, roadmap, source registry, and research;
- international routes.

## Trust rules

- Every material claim must map to product evidence or a cited primary source.
- No fake customers, testimonials, certifications, benchmarks, or adoption claims.
- “Verified” must display issuer, evidence scope, time, ruleset, and limitations.
- Documentation must work without mandatory client-side JavaScript.
- Accessibility, mobile, performance, security headers, and privacy are launch gates.
