Outcome Assurance for AI actions

When an AI says “done”, verify what actually happened.

TimeProofs links agent claims to signed evidence from gateways, receivers, systems of record, and settlement providers—then calculates a clear, reproducible outcome verdict.

TimeProofs is not a timestamping protocol. It does not prove a claim merely existed at a time. It correlates signed evidence from independent systems to determine the current outcome of an AI action.

      Open protocol foundation
      
        
      A shared vocabulary without a proprietary truth claim.
      
      
      
      
      
        
      
          1
          
      Outcome Event
      
          
      One immutable observation with phase, domain type, status, source, claim, and causal parents.
      
        
      
        
      
          2
          
      Evidence Bundle
      
          
      A portable payload containing events, evidence references, issuers, relationships, digests, and proofs.
      
        
      
        
      
          3
          
      Structural Verifier
      
          
      Checks strict JSON, canonical bytes, object digests, causal references, and Ed25519 signatures.
      
        
      
        
      
          4
          
      Verdict Engine
      
          
      Applies a named ruleset without rewriting the original evidence.
