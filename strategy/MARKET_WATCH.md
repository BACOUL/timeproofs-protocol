# Market Watch

Last reviewed: 2026-07-23

## Signals to monitor

### In-chat commerce

- product discovery and checkout inside assistants;
- merchant-of-record and fulfillment boundaries;
- delegated payment tokens and mandates;
- order, return, refund, dispute, and settlement events;
- merchant and payment-provider adoption.

### In-chat non-commerce actions

- apps and connectors that send, create, modify, approve, cancel, or delete;
- agent permissions and user confirmation UX;
- evidence surfaced to the user after execution;
- cross-agent delegation and task completion.

### Enterprise accountability

- demand for business evidence beyond technical traces;
- audit, insurance, dispute, and regulatory requirements;
- system-of-record confirmation patterns;
- human review and exception workflows.

## Strategic triggers

| Signal | Required response |
|---|---|
| Major chat platform exposes portable outcome receipts | Compare semantics; build compatibility profile before duplicating |
| Payment or commerce protocol adds complete settlement/reversal lifecycle | Reassess payment-specific wedge and emphasize cross-domain assurance |
| Direct competitor secures strong distribution | Accelerate open compatibility, system connectors, or category content based on gap |
| Developers reject receiver middleware | Shift toward connector-based evidence collection and platform partnerships |
| Buyers pay for retention/audit rather than protocol | Prioritize managed service while preserving open verifier |
| No willingness to pay after validated demos | Narrow target market or stop before costly infrastructure |
